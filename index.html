PROJECT ORIGINS

In the aftermath of the Great Data Silence of 2024, global weather forecasting models collapsed due to satellite array failures. To fill the void, the STORM_INC initiative was launchedâ€”a distributed, browser-based simulation network designed to reconstruct historical cyclone behaviors and predict future anomalies.

Originally conceptualized in the blocky, low-res terminals of Roblox & Minecraft servers (circa 2016) by the legendary architect ProtoManly, this system has been rebuilt from the ground up using modern web technologies.

Today, STORM_INC stands as the premier tool for simulated meteorology, offering unprecedented fidelity in steering currents, thermodynamics, and radar reconstruction.
